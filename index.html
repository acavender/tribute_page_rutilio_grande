Who was Rutilio Grande?

Father Rutilio Grande García, SJ was a close friend of Archbishop Oscar Arnulfo Romero (assassinated in San Salvador in 1980).

Here are a few key dates from Fr. Grande's life:

  • July 5, 1928: Born in El Paisnal, El Salvador
  • At age 17 (ca. 1945), enters the Jesuit novitiate in Caracas, Venezuela
  • 1950-1953: Professor in a minor seminary in El Salvador
  • 1959: Ordained a priest
  • 1965-1974: Director of social action projects at the seminary in San Salvador
  • 1967-1977: Served (on and off) the parish in Aguilares, El Salvador
  • March 12, 1977: Assassinated by Salvadoran security forces near El Paisnal
You can learn more about Fr. Grande at his Wikipedia page.
